# CONTENT_STRATEGY.md

Content Writing Rules:

Tone:
- Warm
- Professional
- Human
- Elegant
- Concise

Avoid:
- Overly corporate wording
- Long paragraphs
- Generic buzzwords

Hero Headline:
Talent Acquisition & HR Professional Helping Organizations Build Strong Teams

Hero Subheadline:
Experienced in recruitment operations, candidate assessment, and HR administration with strong people-centered communication.

About Section:
Focus on:
- Background in Psychology
- HR journey
- Core strengths
- Working values

Experience Section:
Each experience must contain:
- Role
- Company
- Timeline
- Responsibilities
- Achievements

Achievement Format:
Use metrics whenever possible.

Examples:
- Invited 100+ candidates/day
- Assessed 50–70 psychotests daily
- Time-to-fill under 3 weeks
