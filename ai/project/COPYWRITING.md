# COPYWRITING.md

Copywriting Rules:

Voice:
- Professional
- Warm
- Human
- Confident
- Elegant

Tone:
- Friendly but polished
- Trustworthy
- Clear
- Concise

Writing Style:
- Short paragraphs
- High readability
- Strong headlines
- Avoid jargon overload

Avoid:
- Generic corporate buzzwords
- Robotic wording
- Overly formal language
- Long walls of text

Messaging Principles:

1. Show credibility
Use measurable achievements.

Good:
Successfully invited 100+ candidates in one day.

Bad:
Worked hard to recruit candidates.

2. Focus on impact
Explain outcomes, not only tasks.

Good:
Reduced average hiring time to under 3 weeks.

Bad:
Handled recruitment process.

3. Human-centered communication
Reflect empathy and interpersonal strength.

Hero Copy Formula:
Identity + Expertise + Value

Template:
I help organizations build stronger teams through effective talent acquisition and people-centered HR operations.

CTA Rules:
CTA must be simple and direct.

Examples:
- Connect With Me
- Let's Talk
- Get In Touch
- View Experience

Section Writing Guide:

Hero:
Emotional + professional impact

About:
Personal story + values

Experience:
Responsibilities + measurable results

Skills:
Grouped by expertise

Contact:
Warm invitation

Copy Length:
Hero:
1 headline + 1 subheadline

About:
100–180 words

Experience:
3–5 bullet points each role

Contact:
Maximum 50 words
